About us

From a garage to global impact

Founded in 2022, skillerstech started as a two‑person collective obsessed with logistics and AI. Today we're a remote‑first startup with talent across three continents, serving clients from San Francisco to Singapore.

We don't just write code — we write copy that converts, design systems that scale, and strategies that future‑proof. Our name stands for skill + ers (the people) + technology. Every client gets a dedicated team of engineers, creatives, and consultants.

Our core belief: technology must be human. That's why we embed copywriters from day one — so your product speaks human, not just machine.
